Southampton Nifflers

"We will always find the gold"



The Southampton Nifflers are based in the town of Southampton, which is located in the south of the country. The team was founded in 1603, making them the second-oldest team in the league. The Nifflers are know for their relentless hunt for the gold medal, reminiscent of their tresure hunting namesakes.
